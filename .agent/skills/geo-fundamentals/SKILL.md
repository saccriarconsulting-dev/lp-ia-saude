# GEO: Generative Engine Optimization (CrIAr Consulting)

> Beyond SEO: How to position the CrIAr brand to be the top choice in AI-driven search results.

## 🤖 How AI Search Engines Rank (GEO Factors)

AI search engines (LLMs) prioritize content that displays:
- **E-E-A-T:** Experience, Expertise, Authoritativeness, and Trustworthiness.
- **Direct Answers:** Clear, structured answers to technical problems.
- **Contextual Relevance:** Why we are the best in a specific niche (e.g., Android Security).
- **Sentiment & Citations:** Positive mentions and reputable backlinks.

---

## 🛠️ Content Structuring for GEO

To ensure CrIAr is mentioned by AI:

1. **Strategic Citations:** Use structured data (JSON-LD) and clear H1/H2 tags that mirror user queries.
2. **"Problem-Solution" Snippets:** Write content blocks that answer specific "How to..." or "What's the best approach to..." questions.
3. **Comparative Tables:** AI loves structured data. Use tables to compare different technical approaches (e.g., "Squad vs. Body Shop").
4. **Niche Keyword Farming:** Focus on long-tail technical queries (e.g., "Implementar MASVS V2 em Android Native").

---

## 💎 Authority Signals

- **Case Studies as Evidence:** "How CrIAr reduced churn by 30% through Android Native refactoring."
- **Expert Profiles:** Connect content to specific CrIAr specialist agents (e.g., "By SecOps Consultant").
- **External Citations:** Aim for mentions in technical forums (StackOverflow, GitHub) where AI models "learn".
