# Consulting Pricing & Risk Management

> Methodology for balanced pricing: Market share growth vs. Operational Security.

## 💰 Pricing Logic (Market Share Strategy)

| Phase | Strategy | Margin Target |
|-------|----------|---------------|
| **Phase 1: Market Entry** | Volume-focused, low initial markup. | 15% - 25% |
| **Phase 2: Consolidation** | Brand authority, performance-based. | 25% - 40% |
| **Phase 3: Premium** | High-stakes projects, niche dominance. | 40% - 60%+ |

### Composition Variables
- **Base Fee**: (Direct Hours x Hourly Rate) + (Tools/Infra).
- **Seniority Mix**: Cost per role (Pleno, Senior, Specialist).
- **Risk Buffer**: 5% to 20% addition based on risk score.
- **Management Fee**: ~10% for PM/Discovery overhead.

---

## 🛡️ Operational Security Veto (CrIAr Standard)

A proposal **MUST** be vetoed or re-scoped if any of these conditions are met:
1. **Incompatible Timeline**: Deadline > 20% shorter than estimation.
2. **Ambiguous Scope**: No clear acceptance criteria or deliverables.
3. **High Integration Risk**: Critical dependency on unstable third-parties with no fallback.
4. **Under-dimensioned Team**: Selling Pleno work for Specialist complexity.
5. **Legacy Blackbox**: Un-audited legacy code with fixed-price SLA.

---

## ⚡ Risk Matrix

| Risk Factor | Weight | Threshold |
|-------------|--------|-----------|
| **Stack Novelty** | 15% | High if team never used the tech. |
| **Integration Complexity** | 25% | High if > 3 external APIs. |
| **Client Environment** | 20% | High if no staging/dev environment. |
| **Scope Maturity** | 40% | High if only "ideas" exist without spec. |

**Score > 70% → Require mandatory Discovery phase before final Pricing.**

---

## 🛠️ Pricing Checklist
- [ ] Is the **Seniority Mix** correct for the complexity?
- [ ] is there a **Management Overhead** included?
- [ ] Does the price allow for a **Security Margin** for rework?
- [ ] Has the **Operational Security** been validated by a Technical Lead?
