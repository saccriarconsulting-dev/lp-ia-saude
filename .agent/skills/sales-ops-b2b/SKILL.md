# Sales Ops & B2B Indicators

> Management of the commercial funnel, metrics, and indicators for B2B technology consulting.

## 📈 Commercial Funnel (CRM Stages)

| Stage | Definition | PASS Criterion (Commercial → Technical) |
|-------|------------|-----------------------------------------|
| **Lead / MQL** | Marketing Qualified Lead identified. | Service fit (Target profile). |
| **Discovery** | Understanding problem/needs. | Budget + Decision Maker availability. |
| **Assessment** | Technical analysis of feasibility. | Scope documented + Technical approval. |
| **Proposal** | Documented commercial offer. | Pricing + Timeline + Risk SOW. |
| **Negotiation** | Final adjustments to SOW/Price. | Contractual/SLA consensus. |
| **Closed Won** | Signed contract. | Ready for Handoff. |
| **Closed Lost** | Lost to competitor or stalled. | Documented reason (Price, Tech, Timing). |

---

## 📊 Core Indicators (KPIs)

### 1. Acquisition & Conversion
- **CAC (Customer Acquisition Cost)**: Selling expenses / New customers.
- **Win Rate**: Closed won / Total opportunities.
- **Cycle Time**: Days from Discovery to Closed Won.

### 2. Relationship & Growth
- **LTV (Lifetime Value)**: Total revenue expected from a client.
- **Expansion Rate**: Existing clients buying more services.
- **Churn Rate**: Clients stopping consulting services.

### 3. Efficiency
- **Ticket Médio**: Average proposal value.
- **Margin by Project**: Actual profit vs. Estimated.
- **Technical Loss Rate**: Opportunities lost due to technical unfeasibility.

---

## 🔮 Forecast & Maturity

A forecast is not a guess; it's a technical projection.
1. **Commercial Heat**: How much the client wants/needs the solution.
2. **Technical Maturity**: How well we understand the "How".
3. **Probability of Success**: (Heat x Maturity).

**Forecast Categories:**
- **Commit**: 90%+ probability.
- **Strong**: 70% probability.
- **Pipeline**: <50% probability, needs more discovery.

## 🛠️ Sales Ops Checklist
- [ ] Is the **Win Rate** healthy for our target segment?
- [ ] Are we losing too many leads due to **Technical Inviability**?
- [ ] is the **Handoff** process being followed for every "Won" deal?
- [ ] are the **Reasons for Loss** tracked to improve future proposals?
