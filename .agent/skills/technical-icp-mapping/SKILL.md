# Technical ICP Mapping (CrIAr Consulting)

> Methodology for identifying the Ideal Customer Profile based on technology signals and organizational maturity.

## 🎯 Technical Segmentation Matrix

| Signal | Indicator | Qualification Level |
|--------|-----------|---------------------|
| **Stack Maturity** | Legacy Core (Java 8, SOAP) | High Support Demand. |
| **Growth Signal** | Active hiring for "Kotlin/Android" | Squad/Allocation Demand. |
| **Cloud Moment** | Recent migration or "Cloud-native" focus | Modernization/Performance Demand. |
| **Security Risk** | Financial/Health sector w/ unregulated apps | SecOps/MASVS Demand. |
| **Team Size** | < 10 devs (Growth) or > 50 devs (Enterprise) | Squad vs. Specialized Project. |

---

## 🔍 "Moments of Change" Detection

The Marketing Analyst looks for specific events that trigger high-value demands:
- **Product Launch:** High need for speed and scalability.
- **Funding Round:** Pressure for rapid team growth (Squads).
- **Security Breach (Market news):** Need for immediate security audit.
- **Tech Debt Crisis:** Stagnant apps in the store (High support demand).
