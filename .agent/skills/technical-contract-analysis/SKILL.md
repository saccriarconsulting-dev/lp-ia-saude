# Technical Contract Analysis (CrIAr Consulting)

> Methodology for reviewing the technical side of commercial contracts to prevent operational loss.

## 🛡️ Critical Clauses Review

| Clause | AE Check | Operational Impact |
|--------|----------|---------------------|
| **SLA (Service Level)** | Is the uptime/response time achievable? | Risk of fines if too aggressive. |
| **Acceptance (Aceite)** | Is there a time limit for client review? | Risk of "eternal project" if no limit. |
| **Scope Changes** | Is there a clear CC (Change Control) process? | Prevention of Scope Creep. |
| **Penalties** | Are they capped at a reasonable %? | Financial safety. |
| **Post-Go-Live Support** | Is the period defined (30, 60 days)? | Resource allocation planning. |
| **Responsibility** | Obligation of Means (Esforço) vs. Result. | Risk of lawsuit on unachievable results. |

---

## 📊 RACI (Commercial-Operational)

Define clearly in the SOW/Contract:
- **Responsible**: CrIAr (Code, Architecture, Deployment).
- **Accountable**: Client Product Owner (Scope approval).
- **Consulted**: Client IT Team (Infrastructure).
- **Informed**: Stakeholders.

---

## 🛠️ Contractual Checklist

- [ ] Does the **SLA** match our team's support capacity?
- [ ] is the **Acceptance Period** defined (Standard: 5 to 10 days)?
- [ ] are the **Penalties** restricted to 10-20% of the milestone?
- [ ] Is there **Zero Tolerance for Losses** (Art. 3 of Project Rules)?
- [ ] Has the **Commercial Director** reviewed the financial risk?
- [ ] Is the **Handoff to Delivery** documented as a contractual requirement?
