# Technical Positioning & Content (CrIAr Consulting)

> Standard for creating high-conversion, strategic, and practical content for IT consulting.

## 🎙️ Tone of Voice & Style

| Attribute | Execution Rule |
|-----------|----------------|
| **Visionary** | Focus on the future of tech (AI, Automation, Security trends). |
| **Strategic** | Connect technical decisions to business growth and risk mitigation. |
| **Practical** | Use examples, code snippets, and real architectural patterns. |
| **Useful** | Every piece of content must solve a small problem or provide a new perspective. |

---

## 📄 Content Types

### 1. The "One-Pager" (Service Overview)
- **Problem**: The specific pain point (e.g., app performance).
- **Solution**: How CrIAr solves it (e.g., Native Android refactoring).
- **Benefit**: The ROI (e.g., -30% infrastructure costs).
- **Proof**: 1 key study or metric.

### 2. Technical Use Cases
- Detailed story of a specific problem, the expert's approach, and the final architectural result (Mermaid diagrams).

## 🛠️ Content Checklist

- [ ] Is the content **Visionary** but **Useful**?
- [ ] Does it target a specific **Technical Pain**?
- [ ] are there **Practical** takeaways for the reader?
- [ ] is it optimized for **GEO** (Generative Engine Optimization)?
- [ ] Does it have a clear **Call to Action** linked to a CrIAr service?
